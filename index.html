Clio + Calliope

Founded in New Orleans, Clio + Calliope provides full-stack web development services to both start-ups and established businesses. We specialize in developing strong relationships with local small businesses, social enterprise, and non-profits.

Our founder, Sia Karamalegos, has over 13 years of experience in strategy, project management, and operations. She graduated from Texas A&M University with a BS in chemical engineering, and received her MBA from Harvard Business School.
